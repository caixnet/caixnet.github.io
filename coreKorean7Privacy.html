Collected information and usage

By using Corea Korean keyboard - Doremi, you consent to the collection and use of your Personal Information by Corea Korean keyboard - Doremi consistent with applicable data protection law and this Privacy Policy.
We ARE NOT sharing Personal Information
(e.g., name, address, telephone number, email address, location, device ID, contacts and personal device's user-dictionary),

Corea Korean keyboard - Doremi collects and uses information from you in several ways:
Typing behavior is tracked and used to learn new words you use that are not in Corea Korean keyboard - Doremi's pre-built words dictionary.
This Personal Information is stored on the device and is not transferred by Corea Korean keyboard - Doremi to any other device or third party.
Typing behavior is also tracked and used to predict which words you commonly use after other words (n-grams).
Your contacts list is read to help Corea Korean keyboard - Doremi predict typing and correct typing.
You may disable this feature in the Setup process of Corea Korean keyboard - Doremi or in the setting pages.
