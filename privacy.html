We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, so please check back with us periodically. "Personal Information" includes information that alone or when in combination with other information may be used to readily identify, contact, or locate you or a specific individual. Collected information and usage By using Foxyz keyboard, you consent to the collection and use of your Personal Information by Foxyz Keyboard consistent with applicable data protection law and this Privacy Policy. We ARE NOT sharing Personal Information (e.g., name, address, telephone number, email address, location, device ID, contacts and personal device's user-dictionary), and we ARE NOT transferring this Personal Information from your device, or Foxyz Keyboard's storage, to any third party or external device. Foxyz Keyboard collects and uses information from you in several ways: Typing behavior is tracked and used to learn new words you use that are not in Foxyz Keyboard's pre-built words dictionary. This Personal Information is stored on the device and is not transferred by Foxyz Keyboard to any other device or third party. Typing behavior is also tracked and used to predict which words you commonly use after other words (n-grams). Your contacts list is read to help Foxyz Keyboard predict typing and correct typing. You may disable this feature in the Setup process of Foxyz Keyboard or in the setting pages. When Foxyz Keyboard may share collected information Never.
